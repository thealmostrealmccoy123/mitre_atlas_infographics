Acquire Infrastructure

The Acquire Infrastructure tactic involves adversaries buying, leasing, or renting various resources ranging from cloud servers and domains to serverless functions to establish a functional environment for staging and executing their operations. By utilizing these solutions, attackers can effectively blend into legitimate network traffic, bypass security measures like Content Security Policies, and maintain a flexible operational footprint that is difficult for defenders to attribute to a specific acto. This tactic also extends to acquiring specialized components for AI-focused attacks, such as physical countermeasures like adversarial stickers to disrupt sensors and AI development workspaces for the high-compute staging of exploits. This process is like a spy renting a nondescript apartment and buying local clothes; it provides the necessary "home base" and disguise to operate effectively within a target environment without drawing unwanted attention.
