Acquire Public AI Artifacts

As part of the Resource Development tactic, adversaries actively search public sources—such as cloud storage, data repositories, and public-facing services—to acquire critical AI artifacts like software stacks, training data, and model configurations. By obtaining these artifacts, particularly those associated with a victim organization's production environment, attackers gain valuable insights into the target's specific AI tasks and methodologies. Ultimately, adversaries can leverage this acquired information to create proxy AI models or directly craft adversarial data, though accessing these resources may sometimes require them to establish accounts or register with the provider.
