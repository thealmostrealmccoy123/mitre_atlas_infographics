AI Artifact Collection

Adversaries employ the AI Artifact Collection tactic to gather AI-specific components, such as machine learning models, datasets, and telemetry data, often for the purpose of exfiltration or to stage future attacks. To accomplish this, attackers search victim systems and compromised environments to locate and acquire proprietary information, including production models and sensitive training data. This collection process frequently targets sources like model registries, object stores, and software repositories, serving as a precursor to stealing the artifacts or using them to further the adversary's operational goals
