Active Scanning

Active Scanning is a reconnaissance technique where adversaries directly interact with a victim's system, such as probing open ports or service email addresses, to identify AI-related services and tools. By analyzing system responses or automated email replies, attackers can confirm the presence of AI agents and determine their specific capabilities or activation triggers. The intelligence gathered through these active probes allows adversaries to tailor future attacks, identify RAG-indexed targets, and plan the exploitation of the organization's AI infrastructure.
