Phishing

Phishing tactic involve adversaries attempting to move through an AI environment to gain access to and control other systems, such as model registries or training pipelines. To facilitate this movement, attackers may use Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) to act as targeted social engineers, scaling spearphishing efforts to trick users into revealing credentials for privileged systems. In practice, this can involve adversaries leveraging already compromised internal accounts to send malicious phishing emails to other users within the same network, allowing them to pivot and expand their foothold.
